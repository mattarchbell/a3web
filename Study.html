Surviving Semester One

Both Applications Specialists and Cartographic Specialists have the same common first semester with the same courses. There will be a great deal of group projects in the back second half of the semester. Here are some tips to help you navigate group work.

  • GEOM101: You will make a group website together. Making changes at the same time may cause merging conflicts.
  • GEOM102: No group project for this course.
  • GEOM103: Get to know your learning style and maximize it. Communication and working problems out together is important.
  • GEOM104: No group projects.
  • GEOM65: For the group project, coordinate with all other groups and choose one survey format. This will save you a lot of time.
  • GEOM66: Pay attention to the topics and concepts that the instructor emphasizes on in his lectures.
  • GEOM67: Practice, practice, practice! It's a lot of self-learning in this course. Tutor sessions are available via Teams.
  • SURV21: Math concepts may be a little tricky, ask for help early on if you don't understand.
  • Plan: Set a plan on the first day of the assignment, works great while everything is fresh in your head.
  • Communication: Schedule meetings and deadlines for your group to ensure progress and milestones are met.
  • Recognize: Everyone has their strengths and weaknesses.
  • NB: Group members may drop out and you may end up with a smaller group.
